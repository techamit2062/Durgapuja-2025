Our Heritage & Mission

Badamtala Ashar Sangha Durga Puja, located in Kalighat, South Kolkata, is one of the most prestigious and widely admired pujas of the city. Established in 1939, this puja has grown from a local neighborhood celebration into a cultural landmark that represents the spirit of Kolkata‚Äôs festive traditions. Over the decades, it has gained national and international recognition, winning several honors, including the Asian Paints Sharad Shanman, for its excellence in creativity and execution.

What makes Badamtala Ashar Sangha truly special is its ability to combine artistic innovation with deep social messages. Every year, the puja introduces a new theme that resonates with contemporary issues or showcases the essence of Bengal's heritage. From highlighting indigenous crafts and rural lifestyles to presenting global inspirations with a Bengali touch, the pandals stand out for their originality and cultural depth. The idol artistry also reflects this creative vision, blending traditional forms with modern interpretations.

The craftsmanship is complemented by spectacular pandal architecture and imaginative lighting arrangements, which draw thousands of visitors. Handcrafted decorations, natural elements, and innovative use of space make the puja a visual masterpiece. Badamtala's themes often leave visitors reflecting on broader social values, giving the festival a deeper purpose beyond celebration.

Despite its artistic achievements, the puja has always retained its devotional essence. The rituals are performed with reverence, accompanied by the rhythmic beats of the dhak and the fragrance of dhuno during dhunuchi dance. This balance of spirituality and creativity ensures that visitors not only admire the artistry but also feel the sanctity of the occasion.

Today, Badamtala Ashar Sangha Durga Puja stands as a shining example of how tradition and modernity can coexist. It is a festival where art, devotion, and community spirit come together, making it a must-visit pandal for those who wish to experience the grandeur of Kolkata's Durga Puja.

Our mission extends beyond the five days of Puja - we organize cultural programs, support local artisans, and maintain the rich heritage of Bengali traditions throughout the year.
