Our Heritage & Mission

Jagat Mukherjee Park Durga Puja is one of the most beloved community festivals in Kolkata, celebrated with immense enthusiasm and devotion every year. Established decades ago, it has grown into a grand event that beautifully blends tradition with creativity. The puja committee, made up of dedicated local residents, meticulously plans the event, ensuring the pandal design, idol artistry, and cultural programs reflect both heritage and contemporary relevance.

The theme often draws inspiration from social issues, mythology, or artistic innovation, making each year unique and thought-provoking. Traditional clay modeling techniques and eco-friendly materials are prioritized, promoting sustainable celebrations. The festival is a vibrant hub for cultural performances, music, and community gatherings, strengthening social bonds within the neighborhood.

Located in North Kolkata, ASituated in a well-connected area of Kolkata, Jagat Mukherjee Park Durga Puja attracts devotees and tourists alike, offering a heartfelt spiritual experience paired with artistic grandeur. The puja not only celebrates Goddess Durga but also serves as a platform for preserving Kolkata’s rich cultural legacy and fostering community unity.

In 2025, the puja celebrates its 89 year's, The puja committee is also deeply involved in social initiatives, such as organizing blood donation camps, supporting underprivileged children, and contributing to disaster relief efforts. This blend of devotion, artistry, and social responsibility makes Jagat Mukherjee park just a puja pandal, but a symbol of Kolkata's festive spirit and cultural heritage. Whether you’re a first-time visitor or a seasoned pandal-hopper, Jagat Mukherjee Park continues to be a must-visit destination during Durga Puja, offering a mesmerizing fusion of art, devotion, and community spirit.

⭐Theme of 2025"AI আশীর্বাদ না অভিশাপ"

The Jagat Mukherjee Park Durga Puja 2025 presents a thought-provoking theme: "AI আশীর্বাদ না অভিশাপ" (AI: Blessing or Curse). This innovative theme delves into the complex influence of artificial intelligence in modern society, highlighting both its transformative benefits and potential risks. The beautifully designed pandal combines traditional craftsmanship with futuristic elements, sparking meaningful conversations about technology’s role in daily life. Visitors experience a powerful blend of culture and cutting-edge issues, making this Durga Puja a must-visit event in Kolkata. By addressing AI’s ethical dilemmas and societal impact, Jagat Mukherjee Park creates awareness while celebrating the spirit of innovation and tradition.

Traditional Techniques
Jagat Mukherjee Park Durga Puja beautifully blends traditional techniques with modern themes. Skilled artisans use age-old methods like hand-molded clay idols, natural color pigments, and bamboo scaffolding to create the intricate pandal and statues. These time-honored crafts preserve Kolkata’s rich cultural heritage while enhancing the futuristic AI theme, showcasing the perfect harmony between tradition and innovation during Durga Puja 2025.
Natural Materials
Jagat Mukherjee Park Durga Puja emphasizes sustainability by using natural materials like bamboo, jute, clay, and organic fabrics. These eco-friendly resources not only reduce environmental impact but also highlight Kolkata’s commitment to green celebrations. The use of such materials adds authenticity and connects the festival’s futuristic AI theme with nature’s timeless elements, making Durga Puja 2025 a celebration of both innovation and environmental consciousness.
Community Heritage
Jagat Mukherjee Park Durga Puja is a vibrant expression of Kolkata’s rich community heritage. Rooted in decades of tradition since 1936, the puja brings together generations to celebrate cultural values and social unity. The 2025 theme, "AI আশীর্বাদ না অভিশাপ," reflects the community’s thoughtful engagement with modern challenges while honoring their enduring devotion and artistic legacy.
