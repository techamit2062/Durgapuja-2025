About BAIT Institute

At Barrackpore Academy of Information Technology (BAIT), we believe in empowering individuals through practical, industry-relevant computer education. Since our inception, we have been dedicated to nurturing IT skills with a focus on quality, professionalism, and personal growth. Durga Puja Website Guide is fully new & innovative concept Developed by BAIT (Barrackpore Academy of Information Technology) and its talented and knowledgeable students, this app is your one-stop digital companion for celebrating Durga Puja in Kolkata & it's surrounding places.
