Kalyani ITI More Luminous Club
Kalyani ITI More Luminous Club Durga Puja, established in 2011, has quickly risen to fame for its grand, theme-based pandals and magnificent artistry. Known for recreating world landmarks like Grand Lisboa and Wat Arun temple, it attracts huge crowds every year, making it one of Nadia's most celebrated Durga Pujas.

Kalyani Boat Park
Kalyani Boat Park Durga Puja, established in 1998, is one of the most popular pandals in Kalyani. The scenic riverside location adds charm to its grand decorations and creative themes. Visitors enjoy cultural programs, artistic lighting, and vibrant idols, making it a perfect blend of devotion, tradition, and festive celebration.
