Our Heritage

A Festival That
Transcends Time

For over four centuries, Durga Puja has been the heartbeat of Bengal, transforming neighborhoods into open-air art galleries where tradition meets innovation. Each pandal is a masterpiece, crafted by skilled artisans who pour their souls into creating these temporary temples.

From the narrow lanes of old Calcutta to the modern suburbs, pandals serve as cultural amphitheaters where communities come together to celebrate the triumph of good over evil, showcasing themes that range from classical mythology to contemporary social issues.

The Artisans Behind the Magic

In the narrow bylanes of Kumartuli, master craftsmen continue a centuries-old tradition, transforming simple clay into divine masterpieces. These skilled artisans, known as 'Mritshilpi,' inherit their craft through generations, each adding their unique touch to the eternal story of creation.

  • Traditional Techniques ‚Äì Ancient methods passed down through 15+ generations
  • Natural Materials ‚Äì Eco-friendly clay, natural pigments, and straw
  • Community Heritage ‚Äì Supporting 50+ artisan families preserving sacred craft
